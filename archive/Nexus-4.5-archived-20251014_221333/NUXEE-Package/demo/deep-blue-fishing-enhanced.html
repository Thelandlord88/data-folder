Our Charter Services

Half-Day Charter

Perfect for beginners and families. Four hours of guided fishing targeting local species including bass, rockfish, and halibut.

Includes: All equipment, bait, fishing license, and expert guidance

$450 per boat (up to 4 people)

Full-Day Expedition

Our most popular option. Eight hours targeting trophy fish in deeper waters. Lunch and refreshments included.

Includes: Premium equipment, lunch, all licenses, and photo package

$850 per boat (up to 6 people)

Multi-Day Adventure

The ultimate fishing experience. Two to five-day expeditions to remote fishing grounds with overnight accommodations aboard.

Includes: All meals, premium gear, private cabin, and professional crew

$1,800 per person per day

Tournament Prep

Specialized training for competitive anglers. Learn advanced techniques, tackle setups, and tournament strategies.

Includes: One-on-one coaching, tournament-grade equipment, and strategy sessions

$650 per session

Night Fishing Special

Experience the magic of night fishing under the stars. Target squid, shark, and other nocturnal species.

Includes: Specialized night gear, light systems, and evening refreshments

$550 per boat (up to 4 people)

Kids & Beginners

Special introductory sessions designed for children and first-time anglers. Patient instruction in a fun, safe environment.

Includes: Kid-sized equipment, safety gear, and teaching materials

$350 per boat (up to 5 people)
